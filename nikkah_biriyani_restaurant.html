Our Story

Born from Passion, Seasoned by Time

Nikkah Biriyani was born in the heart of Gobichettipalayam in 2024, when our founder Asraf Ali began serving his grandmother's secret biriyani recipe — the same authentic dum cooking style that defines the best biriyani in Tamil Nadu.

What started as a simple dream grew into Gobichettipalayam's most beloved biriyani destination. The name "Nikkah" honours the sacred tradition of community — just as a wedding feast brings people together, our biriyani unites families, friends, and strangers at the same table.

Today, every pot we cook carries the same soul — patience, quality seeraga samba rice, halal ingredients, and the irreplaceable magic of authentic dum cooking.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Nikkah Biriyani, Gobichettipalayam
Where is Nikkah Biriyani located in Gobichettipalayam?
We are located at Bajanai Kovil Street, Pudupalayam, Gobichettipalayam, Tamil Nadu 638476. Call us at +91 7558162286 for directions.
What are the timings of Nikkah Biriyani?
We are open Monday to Sunday from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM. On Fridays we open at 12:30 PM. We are open all 7 days including public holidays.
Does Nikkah Biriyani offer home delivery in Gobichettipalayam?
Yes! We offer home delivery, takeaway, and dine-in services in Gobichettipalayam and nearby areas. Call +91 7558162286 or WhatsApp us to place your order.
Does Nikkah Biriyani do wedding and function catering?
Yes! We provide professional biriyani catering for weddings, receptions, birthday parties, corporate events, and all types of functions across Gobichettipalayam and Erode district. Minimum order is 50 plates and advance booking is required. Call us for bulk order pricing.
Is Nikkah Biriyani halal?
Yes, all meat used at Nikkah Biriyani is 100% halal-certified. We serve authentic Muslim-style dum biriyani made with pure halal ingredients.
What is the price of biriyani at Nikkah Biriyani?
Our prices start at ₹160 for Veg Biriyani, ₹200 for Chicken Biriyani, ₹280 for Mutton Biriyani, ₹260 for Fish Biriyani, and ₹350 for Prawn Biriyani. Chicken 65 starter starts at just ₹50.
What type of rice is used in Nikkah Biriyani?
We use premium aged Seeraga Samba rice — the traditional short-grain rice used in authentic Tamil Nadu and Ambur biriyani. This is what gives our biriyani its distinct aroma and texture.
